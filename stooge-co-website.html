ABOUT US

creativity lives here.....

Founded in 2015, Stooge Co was built on one simple belief: design should make people stop, look twice, and remember. From day one, we've chased bold ideas and crafted visuals that refuse to blend into the background.

Our team is a tight-knit crew of designers and brand strategists who thrive on turning blank canvases into powerful statements. Whether we're polishing a logo, mapping out a full brand identity, or laying out a larger-than-life mural, every project gets the same treatment — sharp thinking, meticulous detail, and a dash of fearless creativity.

Over the years, we've partnered with startups hungry to make their mark and established brands ready for a refresh. No matter the size of the brief, our goal is constant: deliver standout, impactful design that sparks conversation and drives results.
